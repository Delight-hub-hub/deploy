Our Services

At Brick And (PTY) LTD Construction and Projects, we offer a full spectrum of building solutions that bring innovation, durability, and precision to every project we undertake.

Residential Projects

Building a home is one of life’s most meaningful investments, and at Brick And, we take pride in helping our clients turn their visions into lasting realities. Our residential construction services are built on quality, transparency, and a deep understanding of each client’s needs. From single-family homes to modern housing developments, we deliver comfort, safety, and style without compromise.

We handle every stage of residential development — including architectural planning, material sourcing, structural construction, roofing, plumbing, electrical works, and interior finishing. Our experienced team ensures all details are executed with precision and in full compliance with industry standards and local regulations.

Whether you’re building a dream home, adding new rooms, or renovating existing spaces, our team ensures seamless communication and project management from start to finish. Every project is supervised by skilled professionals who guarantee on-time delivery and exceptional craftsmanship.

Our Residential Services Include:

  • New Home Design & Construction
  • Townhouses, Duplexes, and Multi-Unit Housing
  • Home Extensions and Additions
  • Luxury Custom Builds
  • Energy-Efficient and Smart Home Installations
  • Interior Design & Finishing Solutions

Commercial Projects

Our commercial construction services are tailored for businesses that value professionalism, efficiency, and long-term return on investment. We understand the unique demands of the commercial environment — from strict timelines and budget controls to safety and branding requirements. Our team executes each project with precision, ensuring functional, attractive, and compliant business spaces.

Whether it’s an office complex, retail center, restaurant, school, or industrial facility, we focus on building strong, sustainable structures that enhance your business operations. We collaborate closely with stakeholders, architects, and engineers to ensure that your project aligns with your business goals and operational needs.

Our team also specializes in commercial renovations, tenant improvements, and fit-outs — transforming existing buildings into efficient, modern, and appealing spaces that reflect your brand’s identity and purpose.

Our Commercial Services Include:

  • Office Buildings and Corporate Headquarters
  • Retail Stores, Showrooms, and Shopping Centers
  • Educational, Health, and Community Facilities
  • Restaurants and Hospitality Developments
  • Industrial Structures and Warehousing
  • Commercial Fit-Outs and Renovations

Renovations & Upgrades

Whether you’re refreshing your home, upgrading your office, or restoring a property, our renovation and upgrade services are designed to breathe new life into your spaces. We combine technical expertise, creativity, and craftsmanship to transform old or outdated structures into modern, efficient, and visually appealing environments.

We approach each renovation with a focus on understanding the existing structure’s strengths and limitations before proposing improvements. Our solutions blend functionality and aesthetics, ensuring the result is practical, durable, and aligned with your vision.

From partial room upgrades to full property overhauls, we manage every phase — design, demolition, structural work, electrical and plumbing updates, finishing, and final inspection. Our team works efficiently to minimize disruption and complete the project on time.

Our Renovation Services Include:

  • Interior Remodeling (Kitchens, Bathrooms, Living Areas)
  • Exterior Upgrades (Walls, Roofing, Paving, Painting)
  • Full Property Restorations and Modernizations
  • Energy Efficiency and Smart System Upgrades
  • Office and Retail Space Refurbishments
  • Flooring, Ceilings, and Finishing Improvements
